# Proxy Form

Use this form to assign your vote to another person (proxy) when you are unable to attend an HOA meeting. Complete and submit according to the instructions on the form and the requirements in the Bylaws.

For more on voting and proxy procedures, see the [Voting & Proxy](/voting) page.
