# Declaration of Covenants & Restrictions

The Declaration of Covenants, Conditions & Restrictions (2005) and Addendums A through F govern the Crooked Lake Reserve community. This document covers architectural guidelines, property use restrictions, leasing rules, and homeowner responsibilities.

**Current version includes all addendums through 2024.**
