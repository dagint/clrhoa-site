# Annual Meeting Scheduled for February 15, 2026

The Crooked Lake Reserve HOA Annual Meeting will be held on **February 15, 2026 at 7:00 PM** at the community clubhouse.

## Agenda Items

- Review of 2025 financial statements
- Election of board members
- Discussion of 2026 budget
- Q&A session

## Important Information

All homeowners are encouraged to attend. If you cannot attend in person, proxy forms are available in the Documents section. Please submit proxy forms by February 10, 2026.

For questions, please contact the board at board@clrhoa.com.
